Marie Curie Nurses

Our nurses provide high-quality care to people with terminal illnesses in their own homes.

Find out more


Marie Curie gives people with terminal illnesses the choice to die at home. Our nurses provide them and their families with free hands-on care and emotional support, in their own homes, right until the end.
